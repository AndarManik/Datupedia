How Datupedia Works

Step 1: Text Extraction

First, our system combs through Wikipedia, identifying pages that reference your search term. We then isolate the relevant paragraphs linking to your chosen topic, ensuring focused and pertinent content.

Step 2: Text Organization

Next, these paragraphs are transformed into a numerical format using text embeddings. This advanced technique groups similar ideas together and separates distinct concepts, effectively organizing the information hierarchically.

Step 3: Article Generation

Finally, from this organized data, we craft a unique prompt for GPT-4. The resulting output, tailored specifically to your query, includes comprehensive section divisions and hyperlinks, presenting you with a rich, detailed article.
